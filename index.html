Coin & token Validator

This coin validator has set new reference standards in coin validation. With five different sensors and ten recognition parameters, it ensures a high level of recognition and discrimination of valid and counterfeit coins. With the coin validator, the customer can pay with coins and tokens. We can configure the coin validator to accept any currency.

Configure Settings

  • Programmable logic controller

    With built-in high resolution,
    full-color touchscreen &
    onboard I/O configurations.

  • Slide up to access touchscreen

    The touchscreen is protected
    with a PVC screen so the
    customer cannot change the
    (payment) settings.

  • Touch screen to enter Setup Menu

    In the ‘Setup Menu’ you can edit:
    – Total time
    – Minimum Pay (Startprice)
    – Language and currency sign
    – Select ‘Freewash’

  • Test Menu

    In the ‘Test Menu’ you can
    activate and test all programs.
    You can activate and de-activate
    the Coin validator, Banknote
    reader and NAYAX terminal.
